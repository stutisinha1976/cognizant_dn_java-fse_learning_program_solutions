• Explain the need and benefits of component life cycle

The component life cycle allows developers to hook into specific phases of a component's existence (mounting, updating, unmounting) to perform actions like data fetching, cleanup, and DOM updates. It improves performance, enhances control, and ensures cleaner and more maintainable code.



• Identify various life cycle hook methods

constructor(), componentDidMount(), shouldComponentUpdate(), componentDidUpdate(), componentWillUnmount(), componentDidCatch(), getDerivedStateFromProps(), getSnapshotBeforeUpdate()
